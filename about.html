About me and my work:

My name is Elspeth, and I'm a visual artist and graphic designer. My favorite medium is marker, specifically Copic markers. Drawing has always been my first love, and I try to sketch something everyday. As a designer, I'm interested in both print and web design, and how we interact with both on a daily basis.

How did I get here?

After studying Art History and Visual Art at the University of Chicago, traveling, and working in education for a few years, I realized I needed to be creative in my work. The spark: last year, a tarot card reader told me at my friend's bridal shower that I gave her strange, "diagonal energy" and that I was "put on this earth to share my creativity." The timing of this was pretty uncanny as I was debating about registering for my first graphic design class. That night, I did it. And since then, I've been going for my dream of being an artist and designer.
